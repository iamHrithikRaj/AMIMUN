Amity International Model United Nations

The second e-edition of Amity International Model United Nations shall be a simulation of the United Nations, spanning two days. At AMIMUN, delegates gain a better understanding of the inner workings of the UN, engage in diplomatic discussion, and enhance their knowledge of global relations. The past ten editions of AMIMUN have each continued to set the bar higher in terms of innovative themes and constructive debate; with this conference continuing the tradition. The second e-edition of AMIMUN shall endeavour to construct a space within which delegates will develop a more holistic consciousness of the world around them, and enhance critical thinking, public speaking, and leadership skills. Thus, AMIMUN abides by the Preamble to the Charter of the United Nations: “To practice tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbours”.

Absolute Commitment

The Secretariat of Amity International Model United Nations Conference consists of people who have consistently proven themselves as hardworking, committed, and knowledgeable. Each person is selected ensuring, AMIMUN's ideals are upheld and executed with the utmost dedication. The secretariat ensures the smooth running of all the committees while maintaining the highest level of hospitality. Thus, we remain committed to the promise of quality and guarantee an enriching experience.

International Platform

Amity University takes the honour of being an institute where several people from various backgrounds interact and work with one another. AMIMUN staying on the same ideal has created an international platform, where ideas, opinions, and views are propagated. The same is promoted by the participation of international delegates and past collaborations with organizations such as the United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan, Amnesty International, and Doctors Without Borders, to effectively impart the knowledge of global affairs, diplomacy, and international policy to the students and to add authenticity to the experience.

Creativity & Innovation

Amity University strives to promote creativity in terms of education. We believe in the overall development of students through participation in various disciplines and activities.

Brush Up Ingenuity

AMIMUN has successfully created a platform where delegates have had the option to master and refine their strategic abilities. The E-edition AMIMUN will encourage the manner of thinking to create solutions for some grave world issues.

Engaging & Paramount Motions

The AMIMUN fraternity brings forth the most engaging motions aimed to facilitate the highest quality of debate. Deliberation on challenging global issues will sharpen the members with the learnings of the United Nations and assist them with understanding the current world request.

Amity University

Our Vision is to build the country by fortifying the general public through providing collective, collaborative, teaching; so as to be the worldwide leader in skill training and embodying our promise to be the university where Modernity Blends with Tradition.
Our Mission is to provide education, catering to all levels across the ever-evolving sphere of modern events, developing frontier areas of knowledge, learning, research and examination; building up the general character of students. We not only create great experts but well-rounded individuals. Our students understand and have respect for human qualities, pride in their legacy and culture, can discern what is right, and imbibe attributes of courage, conviction and of action.

Academic Excellence

Amity University strives for the uncompromising quality and highest standard of excellence in teaching, learning, research, and scholarship across various disciplines.

Integrity & Ethics

Amity University upholds the highest ethical values, integrity and professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to academic freedom, transparency, and accountability.

Diversity & Mutual Respect

Amity University nurtures an environment of safety, trust & mutual respect and embeds equality & diversity in its Strategy by ensuring that the strategic plans are fair and inclusive.

Expanding Horizons of Knowledge

Amity University is driven by research and innovation and ensures continuous engagement in scholarly activities when in pursuit of change, creativity, and excellence.

Shared Governance

Amity University encourages shared decision-making through a process that rests upon collaborative consultation, open flow of information, diverse involvement, and collective deliberations of all stakeholders.

Social Responsibility

Amity University creates and nurtures an inclusive environment where everyone can develop to their full potential and contribute to the interest of society as a whole.

Environmental Responsibility

Service
