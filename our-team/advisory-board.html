Advisory Board

Mr. Meshuwa Mawthoh
Secretary General
AMIMUN 2021

The Amity International Model United Nations Conference is one of the biggest student-led conferences of Amity University, Noida. It has made an indelible mark for itself in the Indian MUN Circuit and Asia-Pacific MUN Circuit as among the best MUNs to attend in the region. Over the last 11 years, AMIMUN has set a benchmark in terms of the quality of debate as well as the quality of its execution. AMIMUN has a long list of achievers in various fields who have attended and learned so much from it. It is able to proudly boast of providing its participants with the best opportunity any MUN conference could offer.
My attachment with the AMIMUN dates back to 2018 when I participated as a delegate of the 8th edition of AMIMUN, which inspired me to one day reach the rings of leadership within it. I am blessed to have had the honour and privilege to hold the post of the Secretary-General of the first e-edition AMIMUN Conference in 2021. I have gained so much from the conference as a delegate and also as a Secretariat member. I owe AMIMUN a great deal for the organizational skills I possess today.
I am glad to know that AMIMUN is back with the second E-edition on the 22nd-23rd January 2022. The Secretariat this year has chosen a very apt theme for the circumstances we are encountering at hand - Perseverantia Omnia Vincit. In these trying times of the COVID-19 pandemic we have seen the struggles and have encountered ourselves the challenges the pandemic has brought forward, but through it all we as a global community, as a nation and as individuals have persevered through it all and today we are here in the cusp of history as the generations that are surviving a deadly pandemic.
Our world today faces many challenges and the disparity between the accessibility to rights of all individuals regardless of economic factors, age, gender or race is greater than ever. The global community together may fall short of their own Goals set to be met by 2030, therefore it is very important that young people like us come forward and discuss global issues to understand them. An MUN is not just a platform of speaking but of in-depth research and analysis of the agendas at hand, therefore the more awareness we become of global issues the more prepared we are as a race to systematically solve such issues. I, therefore, invite young people throughout the globe to come and be part of the AMIMUN 2022.
I give my heartiest best wishes to Ms Shubhangi Thakur (Secretary General of AMIMUN’22) and her team to make this instalment a grand success and to break the barriers and benchmarks set by previous conferences.

Ms. Simran Malhotra
Charge D'Affaires
AMIMUN 2021

Model United Nations conferences are verbal debates that are a clash of core ideas and their application to the real world. This focus on actionable change in MUNs is something I believe is especially important, as it lays a clear focus on problem-solving instead of abstract, principled debate. Due to this, delegates must focus on viable solutions, making it necessary for them to consider all angles and viewpoints of a problem, building on their problem-solving skills.
I have seen it all in AMIMUN, be it as an OC in my first year of college, leading the Delegate Affairs team in the second, to being the Charge D'Affaires of the conference during the 2021 e-edition. Behind the deliberation and discussion inside the committees, there is a massive team of students that work day and night for months to make it possible.
I know delegates coming in this year will have a great experience as well. Delegates are sure to enter a conference that nourishes them creatively, so they may progress. As an advisor, I will do my best to bring the best of all my experience through constructive suggestions to the team’s table. More importantly, I know that the Secretariat, and all of the Organising Committee will continue to diligently create a conference wherein each participant flourishes. From expanding their worldview to refining their skills as orators, AMIMUN’22 is sure to be a platform that fosters holistic growth in all individuals involved.
I wish the best of luck to Ms Shubhangi Thakur (Secretary General of AMIMUN’22) and her team who are working tirelessly to make this second e-Edition of AMIMUN a success. I wholeheartedly believe in them.



Mr. Siddhant Treasure
Secretary General
AMIMUN 2020

Model United Nations, a platform based on the principles of the world's main peacekeeping entity, the United Nations, is a simulation of how this vast and altruistic organization operates. It is not simply a cacophony of impassioned dialogue, nor is it a tidal wave of numerous debates; rather, it is a clash of fundamental concepts as well as their implementation in the actual world. This aspect of MUNs, focusing on actionable change, is especially important to me because it places a clear emphasis on problem-solving rather than ambiguous debate. This emphasis on problem-solving distinguishes MUNs from other activities because it actively encourages the development of a broad knowledge base based on solid research and the development of clarity of action.
The Amity International Model United Nations has carved out a niche for itself in the domestic circuit as a conference, nay, a platform, a medium through which today's youth are empowered to pursue their own beliefs while being surrounded by the best in terms of hospitality and quality. Having served as the Secretary General for the 9th edition of this Conference, I was able to witness firsthand the long-term benefits of being a part of something much larger than yourself. During my time there, I learned what it meant to be a part of such an amazing team, a team that became family to me.
I am convinced that this edition will build on this immense legacy by becoming a fine example of excellent debate and discourse. I am confident that my juniors have orchestrated an amazing experience for all people involved. It is an honour and a privilege to welcome you all to the second e-Edition of the Amity International Model United Nations Conference, 2022.

Mr. Aakarsh Shrivastava
Deputy Secretary General
AMIMUN 2020

AMIMUN has always advocated for the importance of Model UNs, believing in their ability to effect tangible change at the grassroots level and harnessing the true potential of insightful debates in shaping the leaders of tomorrow. I am extremely proud of the historic heights attained by this prestigious conference.
As someone who used to be a part of the team that works tirelessly behind the scenes to put on this event, I am in awe of the unwavering dedication shown by each and every member of the organising team, who have not let unfavourable circumstances, such as the pandemic, deter their efforts in putting on a Convention like no other. From the theme of the conference, to the wide array of agendas that are incredibly pertinent to the current global atmosphere, the Secretariat and EB has already done their best to lay the groundwork towards constructing a productive conference.
I am truly honoured to serve on the advisory board of one of the country's largest MUNs, with students from all disciplines devoting their time to solving pressing issues. The conference, which is rich in experience and led by some of the best debaters on the circuit, aims to bring out the best in everyone involved and promotes the values of the United Nations while working toward a better future. Any delegate, reporter or caricature artist is certain to benefit from the rich discussion and open atmosphere AMIMUN provides, allowing individuals to step out of their comfort zones and grow as delegates.
Through working with my colleagues, I endeavour to make certain we reach the goals we have set and create a safe learning environment for all. I extend my best wishes and support to the entire team for the upcoming AMIMUN 2022 conference.



Mr. Karan Kanodia
Director General
AMIMUN 2020

While the United Nations is our best hope for a peaceful and free world, Model UN has emerged as one of the most powerful platforms for connecting ground-breaking ideas proposed by brilliant young minds in this rapidly changing world. And I am honoured to be a part of AMIMUN's legacy, which is one of the most prestigious conferences on the MUN circuit.
I've seen it all in AMIMUN, from being a delegate in my first year of college to being an OC member in my second year, leading the marketing team in my third year, and eventually becoming the conference's Director General in the AMIMUN 2020 edition. As someone who has been intimately involved in the inner workings of this Conference, I can't help but feel that AMIMUN itself is a testament to the tenacity and indomitable will of the Secretariat, as well as all the other members, because behind the two days of deliberation and discussion in the committees, there is a large team of students who work around the clock for months to make it possible.
AMIMUN teaches you life skills, such as how to handle various critical situations and how to manage people in order to achieve a common goal, which is to have the best conference by far every year. The delegates arriving this year will have the opportunity to put themselves in the shoes of UN ambassadors, acting as global citizens while drafting resolutions, plotting strategy, and negotiating with both allies and adversaries. As an advisor for the second E-edition of AMIMUN, I will do everything in my power to assist the team in incorporating novel methods and experiences to ensure that this edition is a resounding success.

Ms. Deviena Shrivastava
Secretary General
AMIMUN 2019

"AMIMUN isn't just a conference, it's an emotion," my senior used to say. Model United Nations is a life-changing experience that I have firsthand knowledge of. Using this platform, one can learn how to inscribe their own vision into a collective belief of an intellectual group of people. Model United Nations is an excellent way for young people to learn about the world and problem-solving. We must be willing to collaborate with people of all ages and backgrounds as future world leaders.
I've had the pleasure of working in AMIMUN for three years and have seen how it has helped me grow on every level of my being. This prestigious conference has been a fantastic experience for me, both as a delegate and as a member of the Organizing Committee. My time as Secretary-General instilled in me the values of discipline, duty, and, most importantly leadership. While the Secretariat and Organising Committee is no doubt currently quite capable, I am certain that through this experience they will forge themselves into better, more experienced individuals. This will lead to a conference that will certainly be in a class of its own.
Due to this, I strongly encourage young orators to participate in AMIMUN 2022 and take advantage of the fantastic opportunities it will provide, especially this year when we should all be thankful. The second e-edition of AMIMUN is to be a place of fruitful debate and discussion, wherein delegates and reporters alike will be exposed to a multitude of ideas, allowing them to organically broaden their horizons, and improve upon the core set of skills required to excel at MUNs. Beginners and experienced orators alike, will certainly be benefitted by the exchange that will take place at AMIMUN’22, especially due to the high quality of debate ensured by the EB members. I, too, will work with my fellow advisors, making certain that any changes we postulate add to the calibre of the conference. I wish the best for the entire AMIMUN'22 Organizing Team and Secretariat!



Mr. Yash Bhati
Director General
AMIMUN 2019

It takes a lot of courage to take the stage and present one's views in front of one's peers, share one's ideas, and push forward with zeal for what one believes in, and yet delegates are able to display such blatant courage whenever the opportunity arises. These reenactments of the United Nations Diaspora have the same effect on the brain of a young ambassador as a way to deal with strategy and the assurance of global support for peacekeeping. The Amity Model United Nations is a wonderful opportunity for young people to see how the world works and how it deals with its troubles.
During my tenure at AMIMUN, I was surrounded by a community of capable individuals that consistently improved upon their wide array of abilities. As Director General, I had the honor of watching this group of individuals come together and add to the long legacy we were a part of. To now be a part of the Advisory Board this year, gives me the opportunity to bear witness once again to the magic that takes place behind the well assembled conference. As a member of the Advisory Board and the AMIMUN'22 team, I am confident that AMIMUN will be a fine demarcation of the Delegates' capacity to influence the debate, and that the Delegates will benefit from this exceptional conference.
Throughout the history of AMIMUNs, each iteration has been a step above the last, with each team learning and improving on the insight passed down. I have unshakeable faith in this year’s team as well, knowing without doubt that the Organising Committee is led by an exceptional Secretariat. For all these reasons, it gives me great pleasure to invite you to the next installment of this prestigious conference.
