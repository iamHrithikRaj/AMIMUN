Core Secretariat

Shubhangi Thakur

Secretary General

Ms. Shubhangi Thakur is currently a third-year BTech CSE student. She has been a part of the Model UN circuit for over 10 years. Ms. Shubhangi began her Model UN career in Dubai as a delegate and has amassed an experience of over 70 conferences. Throughout her experience, she has served in various positions in organising committees over the years, from Founder to Secretary General. Despite being from a technical field, her love for humanitarian agendas keeps bringing her back to the circuit. The influence of MUNs for her is lasting, and Ms. Shubhangi truly believes individuals that enter the circuit will leave it better.

Zeeshan Ahmad Fareed

Director General

Mr. Zeeshan Ahmad Fareed has been debating and taking part in MUNs since middle school. Mr. Zeeshan was an active member of the Kolkata MUN circuit and has also organised IMUN- Kolkata, one of India's more prestigious MUN conferences. He is the current president of Amity International Model United Nations Club, and he has served as the Director-General of The Delhi Diplomacy Summit. He has taken part in over 30 conferences in the Delhi circuit and he has carved himself a niche in the Indian committees. Mr. Zeeshan believes that MUNs are not the end goal but are the means to the end goal of a more compassionate world.

Zeba Hussain

Charge D'Affaires

Ms. Zeba Hussain has been a part of AMIMUN since her first day at Amity University, Uttar Pradesh. Being a part of the Delhi MUN Circuit since 2016 she was determined to apply & continue her passion to be a part of the MUN Society even at the University. For her, AMIMUN is akin to a second home. For Ms. Zeba, AMIMUN is where people are shaped & diplomacy reigns supreme. MUNs according to her are an activity to become what one aspires to be in a broad context with great intellect. She is a believer with a firm conviction & that is what has brought her places.

Areeba Basharat

Chef De Cabinet

Ms. Areeba Basharat has been a part of the Srinagar circuit for MUNs since 2015 but oration has been a part of her life even before junior school. She was one of the first MUNers from Srinagar, Kashmir and accepted the role of mentoring the Kashmiri kids of her school from second-year itself. She has been integral to IIMUN Srinagar Chapter across her high school life and has participated in over 30 MUNs. She believes that MUNs are a way of traversing through social barriers, reaching the point of political intellectuality, creating social diplomacy and of course meeting lifelong allies.
