Amity International Model United Nations Club

We are the Amity University MUN Club, a collection of determined students interested in diplomacy and the field of international relations. We are pleased to represent the Amity International Model United Nations (AMIMUN) conference, one of the largest MUNs in Delhi, which is hosted and organised by Amity University. We train rigorously to continuously grow through our experiences, bettering our discussions, and broadening our perspectives. We endeavour to encourage each member of this club to spare no effort and encapsulate the basic values of the United Nations. Due to this, we have a wealth of practical experience and are always led by seasoned MUNers. The talent exists, and the future is clear: We wish to continue our MUN journey by welcoming others and putting faith in our fellow young student diplomats.

Message from President

Zeeshan Ahmad Fareed
President
AMIMUN Club 2022

Dear Delegates, Welcome to the Amity International Model United Nations Club, a platform where you can speak your mind, albeit respectfully. The aim is not to create just a space for MUNers but to create an experience for each member that will leave you a better version of yourself. We often think diplomacy and public speaking are phenomena that are separated from us, but in reality, they are integral to our daily lives.
As an avid debater, I understand that the need to discuss important issues and convince others is human nature. To nurture this very human instinct we present to you the Amity International Model United Nations Club, wherein one should be ready for an unprecedented level of debate and learning.

Stay safe and happy debating!
Zeeshan Ahmad Fareed

Message from Vice President

Rohan Nagpal
Vice President
AMIMUN Club 2022

Welcome to Amity International Model United Nations Club! Cognizant of the Amity vision towards holistic and overall development, the AMIMUN Club is part of the continuous effort to accomplish this goal. We seek to produce leaders of the highest order, by means of engaging them with MUNs as a tool for self-development.
MUNs over the past decade has managed to transform into mainstream extra-curricular activity. The primary reason behind this widespread acceptance is the amount of rigour involved; as MUNs are not only verbal debates but a clash of core ideas and their application to the real world. The AMIMUN Club takes up the initiative to promote the culture of MUNing within the confines of Amity University, by actively engaging students via means of Training Sessions and Intra-MUNs alike. Possessing one of the largest student member bodies, the AMIMUN Club has grown leaps and bounds since its inception in 2012. Not only is this stellar growth a sign of MUNs being accepted widely among the student body, but also of a successful effort to enrich student minds by imbibing the values of MUNs into the student population. We, at the AMIMUN club, will continue to strive towards perfection and are proud to be at the forefront of this mission.

Regards,
Rohan Nagpal

Message from Vice President

Shubhangi Thakur
Vice President
AMIMUN Club 2022

Greetings,
As Amity University's renowned official Model UN club, we take pride in spreading the Model UN discipline among the students of our university's various institutions. Over the past few years, AMIMUN has been responsible for encouraging and preparing outstanding young MUNers, and they have consequently garnered accolades and recognition not only as delegates but also as Executive Board members to multiple Conferences.
We believe in guiding and mentoring our fellow students and peers in the Art of MUNing at the AMIMUN Club. This is the essence of AMIMUN Club. We strive to be the best in order to give the best platforms and opportunities for every individual who wishes to strive forth alongside us. AMIMUN Club is the largest student body at Amity, Noida, and we are very proud of all of our members and what they have accomplished over the years. We help you build your own perspective on global tragedy, which helps you become a better debater and global leader with long-term objectives. These are arguments about real issues that affect real people, this is actual information; and this is what we do. With a deep belief in the United Nations' principles, we want to continue on this path of progress and ambition.

Regards,
Shubhangi Thakur
